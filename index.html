BDM Chatbot

This chatbot is designed to help IITM BS students working on the BDM project. You can raise queries and get appropriate responses related to the BDM project through this chatbot.

Instructions

  • When entering the chatbot, please enter your official email ID in the format: XXfXXXXXXX@ds.study.iitm.ac.in.
  • Then, enter your name.
  • Once you have entered these details correctly, the chatbot will be enabled.
  • You may ask any question regarding the BDM project, such as:
    • How to write a BDM project proposal?
